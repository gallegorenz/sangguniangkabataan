Sangguniang Kabataan

Welcome! The Sangguniang Kabataan is a branch of the local government which is meant to represent the youth in each barangay in the Philippines. The council represents youth who have resided in their barangay for at least one year and registered to vote. It leads the local youth programs in their respective Barangay, Municipality and Province. One of the main goal of the Sangguniang Kabataan is to empower the youth to be one of the driving force for nation building. Here you can checkout the latest about the Sangguniang Kabataan!
